Custom Orders


For custom orders, send us your garment(s), and we will apply the transfer but we need the following info:

*colour and size of transfer:

1. yellow (large, small and extra small)
2. black (large, small and extra small)
3. white (large, small and extra small)

* for positioning of transfer on T-shirt follow steps 4 and 5,

4. large, normally front central.
5. small, extra small, normally left breast or left sleeve.
6, for special styles i.e. polo shirts, leggings, hoodies etc please state which, and positioning

*cost of transfer and application to each garment.
If there is more than one garment please identify each garment by pinning info, 1-6 onto each garment

£10 plus P+P (for large transfer). £7 plus P+P (for small and extra small)

If paying by cheque or postal order, put it into parcel along with garment(s) and info.
