Summary:
The main points of Tree Sprite International are to discover
your full potential and to enhance your mental and physical health
by introducing an image (Tree Sprite Transfer) which gives common ground to the chaotic and diverse nature which is the world today.
Without the use of chemicals

CareNet:
Is not a charity but a project by Tree Sprite International
Carenet Project is a network of homes
that accomodate single people who are physically or mentally ill or are special needs homeless
Also people who are housebound because of media encounters

The accommodation takes the form of a campsite, a shared house or halls of residence or other form of refuge

Money is raised through the sale of t-shirts, badges, 2D art and photography approval by the "TreeSprite Transfer"
